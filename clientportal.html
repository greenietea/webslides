Customer Experience Technology

We offer great service through our people. What can we offer through our technology?

Greenie, October 2017

Problem

Our clients can only progress their claims with the help from our team via emails, phone calls, or meetings. These channels rely on our team and our clients don't have enough transparency over their data nor our work.

Solution

Have a client portal that allows clients access to their claims and our resources.

Features

  • → Simple claim progression

    Clients know what's blocking us from filing their claims.

  • Summary of the past

    Clients know what we have done for them with a repository of past claims, amended returns, invoices, and raw data collected.

  • Identity profiles

    We build identity profiles on our clients both as organsations and as individuals which can be useful in our growth decisions.

  • Data collection with live chat

    Clients may have questions while giving us data, and we can integrate a live chat system to our existing data collection process.

  • Our resources

    We show how we work and how we price and can use this space to cross sell, promote partner offers and serve relevant content.

  • Add-on services

    We can add a widget to show results from the Grant Matching Tool or other tools that allow us to engage clients in more capacity than tax credits.
